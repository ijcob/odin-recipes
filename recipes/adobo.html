Chicken Adobo

Ingredients:

  • Salt
  • Whole Pepper
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Chicken Thighs
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Dried Bay Leaves

Steps:

  1. In a big pan, stir fry the garlic in oil until golden brown, add the salt and whole pepper.
  2. Add the chicken, give it a bit more of a stir fry until browning.
  3. Add your soy sauce and water, as well as the bay leaves, cover and let simmer.
  4. Now add your vinegar and let it simmer for 15-30 minutes, do not stir.
  5. Cook your chicken until tender, add water as needed.
  6. Once chicken is tender serve on a nice dish, enjoy.
