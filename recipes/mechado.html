Beef Mechado

Ingredients:

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Soy Sauce
  • Potatoes
  • Beef Cubes
  • Carrots
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Dried Bay Leaves

Steps:

  1. Marinate your beef in soy sauce, garlic and dried bay leaves.
  2. In a large wok, sautee in oil the onions, tomato and garlic. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Add your beef and stir until browned then add your tomato sauce.
  4. Add water until beef is submerged, let it simmer until tender.
  5. Add potatoes and carrots and simmer until tender.
  6. Once sauce is thick, serve in a nice dish, enjoy.
