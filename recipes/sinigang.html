Pork Sinigang

Ingredients:

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Tamarinds/Tamarind Mix
  • Pork Belly
  • Water
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Other Veggies

Steps:

  1. In a deep pot, fill with water and add your pork, tomatoes and onions.
  2. Add some salt and pepper, bring to a nice rolling boil.
  3. Once it is boiling, bring to a simmer and let it cook for a 30 minutes.
  4. Add water when necessary, add your tamarinds/tamarind mix.
  5. Once pork is tender, add your veggies and cook until soft.
  6. Serve in a nice big dish bowl, enjoy.
